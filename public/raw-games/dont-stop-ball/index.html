How to play

Dont Stop Ball is a survival dodger about calm movement. The ball now glides with more weight, every hit costs one of three lives, and later stages stack bars, split gates, sweepers, and spinning hazards with less mercy.

Controls

  • Keyboard: Left and Right arrows or A and D
  • Mouse: move across the canvas to pull the ball into line
  • Touch: drag on the canvas or hold the mobile D-pad buttons
  • Pause: press P or tap the pause button
  • Start or restart: press Space, Enter, tap the overlay, or tap the canvas
Power-up mix

Shield soaks a hit, Slow-Mo buys space when the screen gets crowded, and Ghost turns one panic moment into a clean recovery line.

Stage ramp

Warmup and Heated teach the read. Frenzy introduces spinners, then Overdrive, Redline, and Meltdown keep shrinking the safe timing window.

Survival tip

Let the ball settle between moves. The smoother steering rewards short corrections more than wide panic swings, especially when invincibility frames are gone.

What It Is

High-pressure dodge survival.

Dont Stop Ball is a one-more-run dodge game about reading the drop, using three lives well, and reaching the hotter stage bands without wasting a recovery tool.

Controls

Three ways to steer.

Use keyboard, mouse, or touch drag. On mobile, the extra D-pad lets you hold cleaner side steps through busy waves and late spinners.

Tip Or Challenge

Reach Redline with all lives.

Try to save Slow-Mo for Frenzy or later. If you can hit Redline on a live streak, the score will usually take care of itself.
