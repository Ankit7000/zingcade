What Zingcade collects

Zingcade is a small static browser-game arcade and does not currently offer account registration or user profiles. Some game pages may store simple local data in your browser, such as saved best scores or progress markers, so the games can remember your latest results on the same device.

Cookies and similar storage

Zingcade may use cookies, local storage, or similar browser storage for basic site operation, game-state saving, and future site measurement. If cookies are used, they may help remember preferences, improve repeat visits, or support third-party services described below.

Analytics

Zingcade may use lightweight analytics in the future to understand overall traffic, popular pages, and general gameplay interest. If analytics tools are enabled, they may collect non-sensitive usage information such as page views, browser type, approximate device information, and referral data.

Third-party advertising and Google AdSense-ready disclosure

Zingcade may display third-party advertising in the future, including advertising services such as Google AdSense. If third-party advertising is enabled, advertising partners may use cookies or similar technologies to show ads based on visits to this site and other sites. Google and its partners may use advertising cookies to serve ads based on prior visits. Users may be able to manage ad personalization through Google ad settings and other browser or device controls.

External services and updates

Because Zingcade is small and evolving, this privacy page may change as the site adds analytics, advertising, or other standard website features. When those features change materially, this page should be updated to reflect the current setup more accurately.
