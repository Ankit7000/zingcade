How to play

Color Crown is a control game. Your starting color is safe. Push into neutral space to draw a gold trail, then reconnect to your territory to convert the enclosed area.

Controls

  • Arrow keys or WASD change direction
  • Swipe on the arena or use the D-pad on phones
  • Space or Enter starts and restarts
  • P pauses the run
Risk rule

Your trail is vulnerable until it reconnects. If a cutter hits it, the run ends. If you close the loop, the trail becomes land.

Score rule

Score comes from captures, survival time, and total control. Large loops score better, but greedy routes give cutters more time.

Rank path

Best score, captured percent, and largest claim feed the cabinet rank preview and Zingcade profile progression.

What It Is

Single-player territory control.

Claim the map by cutting loops through neutral space, then defend your line while the arena keeps moving.

Core Tension

Safe color, dangerous trail.

Short loops are steady. Big loops win runs. The trick is knowing when to turn back before your trail gets clipped.

Challenge

Reach 50% control.

Half-map control is the first real mastery line. From there, every greedy expansion has less room to breathe.
