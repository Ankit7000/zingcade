How to play

Sky Hop is a vertical climber with gentler catches and sharper score pressure. The jump arc now rises longer than it falls, landings forgive more near the edges, and later climbs mix seven pad types while the sky changes color around you.

Controls

  • Keyboard: Left and Right arrows or A and D
  • Mouse: move across the canvas to steer the hop line
  • Touch: drag on the canvas or hold the mobile D-pad buttons
  • Pause: press P or tap the pause button
  • Start or restart: press Space, Enter, tap the overlay, or tap the canvas
Platform mix

Normal, drift, boost, tiny, crumble, spring, and vertical pads all ask for different setup timing. Above 200m, even the safe pads start shrinking.

Combo pressure

The multiplier only holds while the combo bar is alive. Clean landings in quick succession now matter as much as raw height.

Sky Hop tip

Save hard corrections for the way down. The lighter rise and heavier fall make early shaping stronger than late steering, especially on vertical pads.

What It Is

Combo-driven vertical climbing.

Sky Hop is a browser climber about reading the next landing, chaining pads fast enough to keep multiplier alive, and surviving into the harsher upper zones.

Controls

Keyboard, mouse, and touch.

Tip Or Challenge

Carry a combo past 200m.

Try to keep the bar alive once platforms start shrinking. Spring pads can rescue a climb, but crumble and vertical pads punish hesitation fast.
