How to play

Sky Hop is a climber with gentle landing forgiveness. Your job is to guide the fall, catch the next platform while descending, and stay ahead of the screen as the climb rolls into new altitude zones.

Controls

  • Keyboard: Left and Right arrows or A and D
  • Mouse: move across the canvas to pull the hop line sideways
  • Touch: drag left and right on the canvas
  • Pause: press P or tap the pause button
  • Start or restart: press Space, Enter, tap the overlay, or tap the canvas
Platform mix

The climb starts readable, then adds drift pads, boost pads, tiny pads, and crumble pads. Each one changes how early you need to line up.

Progression hook

Height milestones give each run a concrete target. Your best height is saved locally, and the page always tells you which marker is next.

Sky Hop tip

Start correcting before the peak. The improved air control is smoother, but the best runs still come from shaping the next landing early.
