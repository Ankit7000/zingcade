How to Play

You start in the center lane. Obstacles spawn at the horizon and rush toward you. Shift lanes, jump over barriers, slide under laser beams, and collect coins before the city overwhelms you.

Controls

  • A / Left arrow - shift left lane
  • D / Right arrow - shift right lane
  • W / Up arrow - jump over barriers
  • S / Down arrow - slide under beams
  • Swipe left, right, up, or down on mobile
  • P - pause or resume
  • Space / Enter - start or restart
Obstacle Types

Barrier - jump over it. Laser Beam - slide under it. Full Block - change lane. Double - two lanes are blocked, so find the clear one.

Power-Ups

Shield gives one free hit. Magnet pulls nearby coins. Boost gives a short speed burst with temporary invincibility.

Stage Flow

City Night, Storm Grid, Neon Surge, then Overdrive. Each stage raises speed, tightens spacing, and adds harder obstacle combos.

What It Is

Perspective 3D endless runner.

The track rushes toward you in full perspective, so obstacles scale up from the horizon and demand instant decisions.

Key Move

Lane shift beats everything.

When in doubt, shift early. Jumping and sliding are counters, but lane position is still your safety net every run.

Challenge

Survive Overdrive for 10 seconds.

At max stage the obstacle gap drops below 0.8 seconds. Pattern reading replaces reaction speed entirely.
