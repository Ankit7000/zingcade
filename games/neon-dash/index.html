How to play

Neon Dash is a three-lane endless runner. Read the lane shape, move early, then decide whether the moment wants a lane shift, a jump, or a slide.

Controls

  • Up/W: move higher, or jump on a fast second tap
  • Down/S: move lower, or slide on a fast second tap
  • Left/A: move to the higher lane
  • Right/D: move to the lower lane
  • Touch: swipe up or down, or use the on-screen buttons
  • Mouse: click above or below the runner to shift lanes
  • Pause: press P or use the pause button
  • Start/restart: press Space, Enter, or tap the overlay
Tips

Double barriers demand fast reading, while low blocks and high beams test whether you kept your action timing available. Keep one lane move in reserve when the speed rises.

Stage flow

City Night opens the route, Storm Grid tightens reaction windows, Neon Surge speeds the obstacle mix, and Overdrive pushes the runner toward zero-mercy spacing.

Power-up read

Shield gives one free mistake, Magnet pulls nearby coin lines, and Boost turns the runner into a short invincible rocket. Save clean lines for the moment a timed power lands.

What It Is

A lane runner with sharp reactions.

Neon Dash is a side-scrolling endless runner built around three-lane reads, quick action choices, and short, high-pressure repeat runs.

Controls Summary

Shift first, act second.

Use A/Left and D/Right for clean lane switches, then use Up/W and Down/S when the route wants a jump or slide.

Tip Or Challenge

Try to hold 50+ coins in one run.

Magnets help, but the real gain comes from staying centered before a coin line arrives so you never spend a lane move just to recover.
