How to play

Dont Stop Ball is about reading the warning beam early, gliding into the safe lane, and holding composure as the game mixes single bars, split gates, heavy blocks, and sweeping lines.

Controls

  • Keyboard: arrow keys or WASD for smooth steering
  • Mouse: move across the canvas to pull the ball into position
  • Touch: drag anywhere on the canvas
  • Pause: press P or tap the pause button
  • Start or restart: press Space, Enter, tap the overlay, or tap the canvas
Difficulty ramp

Warmup teaches the read. Heated and Frenzy tighten the spawn gaps. Overdrive and Redline start stacking patterns and faster sweepers.

Replay hook

A dodge chain turns steady survival into score pressure. The next chain target is always visible, so each run gives you a concrete goal besides just lasting longer.

Survival tip

Stay in the lower half, then make shorter corrections than you think you need. The smoother steering rewards gentle adjustments over last-second snaps.
