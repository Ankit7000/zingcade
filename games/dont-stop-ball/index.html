How to play

Survive as long as possible while hazard bars drop from above. Incoming lanes flash before they become solid, so quick reads matter as much as movement.

Controls

  • Keyboard: arrow keys or WASD
  • Mouse: move across the canvas to pull the ball
  • Touch: drag anywhere on the canvas
  • Pause: press P or use the pause button
  • Restart: press Space or Enter after a run ends
Difficulty ramp

The game shifts through Warmup, Heated, Frenzy, and Overdrive. Spawn timing tightens and hazards fall faster in each stage.

Survival tip

Stay near the lower third of the field so you can read warnings early and still have room to slip around wide bars.

Quick loop

Start fast, fail clearly, restart instantly. The whole page is tuned to keep the playfield front and center.
