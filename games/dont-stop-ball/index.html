Dont Stop Ball

Move with arrow keys or WASD. You can also use the mouse or drag on touch screens. Avoid the incoming blocks and survive as the game speeds up.

How to play

Your ball follows both direct input and pointer movement, so you can quickly switch between keyboard and touch-friendly controls.

Controls

  • Keyboard: arrow keys or WASD
  • Mouse: move over the canvas to guide the ball
  • Touch: drag anywhere on the canvas
  • Restart: press Space, Enter, or use the restart button
Difficulty:

The longer you last, the faster the hazards fall and the more often they appear.
